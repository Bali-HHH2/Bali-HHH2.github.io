Run #1520

Date: Saturday 29 January 2022

Time: 4PM Start Time

Location: Bali Budaya Cultural Centre, Br. Batusepih, Kemenuh

This week, Nightjar’s 84th Birthday Run and Bali Hash House Harriers 2’s 30th Anniversary Run. The long will be a flat 8.5-9k 1:45min walk, and the short 4.5-5k walk, less than an hour walking.

After the run, DJ Super Nova will spin up some tunes for us, there will be plenty of Food, Free Flow Grog all night, a Bali Moon Cocktail Bar PLUS lot’s of Free Giveaways.

The run fee will be 150K for adults and 50K for kids. Toilet Trasher and Harelip will be on hand to help with parking. Please note, there will be NO parking on the grass at the venue. Parking will be at the temple nearby and along the road, but all cars must keep to one side of the road.

Hares: Worm. Mount n Groan, Inflatable Bedmate and Short Shaft
