BHHH2 Next Run Map

Run #1380

Location: Goa Gajah, Bedulu

Date: 7-Jul-18

Start Time: 4:00PM

Hare: Team America

GPS: 8°31’22.0″S 115°17’16.8″E

Last weeks run at Sobangan was a excellent example of a well set run. There was enough paper, it was scenic and best of all it was not an extreme run, which made the entire experience much more pleasant. The circle was a great affair with some immediate on-ice time for the sinners in the group. We welcomed a few virgins and overall everyone had a great time.  Thanks to everyone for a great turn out.

This week we have our annual United States of America Independence Day Run which will be held on Saturday the 7th of July at Goa Gajah.  This year Team America consists of Blow Joe, Serial Offender, Jenny Two Mellons, Pussy Delivery & Marble Balls with support from St Tits and all of the other ex pat Sepos that we forgot to mention. So if you know any Americans here in Bali be sure to mention Bali Hash House Harriers 2 is having the annual Independence Day run.

Come walk or run with us along one of two scenic trails around Goa Gajah.  Featuring a Short trail ~6K and Long trial ~10K, enjoy some American style food, free flow Bintang beer and assorted soft drinks.  We will also have a lively hash circle with great friends starting at 6PM and lasting until it ends.

Visiting hashers are more than welcome, we have a mixed family friendly hash here in Bali so everyone is welcome. Get some exercise, see parts of Bali that you might not have seen before and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow Hash House Harriers while you are in Bali.

BHHH2 Next Run Map #1379 Pura Desa Sobangan

June 25, 2018/0 Comments/in Next Run Map /by Bali Hash 2 Hare Raiser

Bali Hash House Harriers 2

Next Run Map

Run #1379

Location Pura Desa Sobangan

Date: 30-Jun-18

Start Time: 4:00PM

(Note please try to arrive before the actual start time)

Hare: Super Size Me

GPS: 8°30’02.5″S 115°11’41.8″E

Last week had a wet but enjoyable run at Desa Punggul with the short being about 6KM and the long at about 10.5KM.  The on out took us down by the subak where we turned south and continued on to the split. The weather, if a bit wet, was cool and refreshing with a slight breeze.  Perfect running and walking weather.  Circle was doused by rain a bit but it soon stopped and everyone had an enjoyable evening.

This week we will go to Pura Desa Sobangan. It’s about 8KM North of Mengwi up the road toward Bedugul. Turn right at the big tree just before the Coca Cola Amatil factory and follow the signs until you get to the run site which is just off the corner with a large wantilan. This is a great area for runs and has a nice variety of ups and downs with rice paddies and beautiful panoramic views.

Try to plan to get to the run site a bit before the actual start time so you can attend the pre-run briefing. Also, we can’t provide detailed information on the exact distance a run site is from some certain place on Instant messenger right before the run.  If you send us an IM right before the run, chances are we’re already on the way to the run site ourselves and will not see that message until Sunday or Monday.

We publish our weekly maps by email, on our website and on social media (Sign up for email, Like us on Facebook, follow us on Instagram, subscribe to our feed on Twitter or check out our profile on Google+). We include GPS coordinates and Google Maps links with every map.  But it’s up to you, the reader, to click on the links.
