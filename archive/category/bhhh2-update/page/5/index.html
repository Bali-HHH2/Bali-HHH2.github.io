UPDATE: Run #1358 Temukus, Buleleleng

(sometimes spelled Temoekoes)

This is a special run located up North in Bali just west of Lovina in celebration of Victor “Nightjar” Mason’s 80th Birthday.  Seeing as how it’s about a 3 hour drive (approximately 90 km from Denpasar) to get there (and so presumably something like a 3 hour drive to get back), we do not advise you try to drive there and back on Saturday.

So if you are going, it would be advisable to make some sort of arrangements for overnight accommodation. That being said, there are a number of accommodations in and around the HQ at Hotel Pantai Mas so it should not be too much of a drama to make arrangements, but time is running out.

There will be a Great Run, of course, followed by a Great Circle, followed by a Great Birthday party for the Great Grand Master of Bali hash House Harriers 2.

Run #1358

Date: 3-Feb-18

Start Time: 4:00PM

Hare: Nightjar & The Usual Suspects

Run Site: Hotel Pantai Mas, Temukus, Lovina, Buleleleng

GPS: 8°10’10.6″S 115°00’25.7″E

-8.169602, 115.007135

Directions: Find your way up to downtown Lovina. Head West out of Lovina on the main road towards  Gilimanuk. You’ll find the BHHH2 run HQ at Hotel Pantai Mas on the right a couple of klicks after you pass Pantai Lovina.  It’s just that easy, so come join us!

On On to Temukus
