Bali Hash 2 Next Run #1488 Cancelled

December 16, 2020/0 Comments/in Next Run Map /by Bali Hash 2 Hare Raiser

Bali Hash 2 Next Run #1488 Cancelled

Bali’s Governor Wayan Koster issued a circular memorandum Tuesday, 15 December 2020, (Nomor 2020 Tahun 2020) addressing public activities over the year-end holiday period effective from 18 December 2020 through 4 January 2021.

Every individual, entrepreneur, manager, event operator, or person in charge of a location or public facility during the Christmas and New Year Period is required to follow prohibitions on public gatherings; limit physical interactions and maintain physical distancing; limit activities involving public places; and accept that it is strictly forbidden to become intoxicated.

Therefore, Bali Hash House Harriers 2 is cancelling all runs between the above-mentioned dates. Our next planned run will be the AGM run on the 9th of January 2021, however we will revisit this 4 January 2021 after we get more updated information.

Regarding the AGM, we would like to hear from interested individuals who would like to volunteer as Hash Master for the 2021 Committee. Please contact any of the current Committee members to express your interest in becoming the next Bali Hash 2 Hash Master.

Sincerely,

Bali Hash House Harriers 2 Committee
