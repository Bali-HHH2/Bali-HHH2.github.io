This will be the climax of all past Worms Burnt!!!

Bali Hash 2 and Dewata Hash proudly present Burning Worm 2023. We start off our weekend on Saturday 22-July with a regular hash run around Baturiti area followed by Circle. The culmination of the evening after circle will be the BURNING WORM!
