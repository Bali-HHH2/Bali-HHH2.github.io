AGM 2020 Election Rules

December 14, 2019/0 Comments/in Special Events /by Bali Hash 2 Hare Raiser

AGM 2020 Election Rules

Dear Bali Hash 2 Members as you may know, the AGM will be this coming January 4th 2020, just 1 week away.  IN AN EFFORT TO SMOOTH THE TRANSITION TO THE NEW COMMITTEE, WE ARE ASKING FOR A VOLUNTEER TO HARE ON JANUARY 18th.

The following are the rules of the road for this AGM:

  • Nominations for Hash Master
    • Hash Master nominations to be declared and seconded by fellow Bali Hash 2 members 1 week prior to the vote (i.e., no later than the 28th of December 2019)
    • Nominees for Hash Master must not be an active Committee member of another kennel
    • Nominees for Hash Master must have been a Bali Hash 2 Member for a minimum of 2 years
    • Nominees for Hash Master should have prior Bali Hash 2 Committee experience
  • Election Process
    • Members of record with Bali Hash 2 are eligible to vote, visitors and virgins are not eligible to vote
    • Pre-printed ballots will be available with the names of the candidates prior to the run
    • Active out-of-country members can vote in advance via email
      • We will use the hash cash roster to develop the list of eligible voters
    • The candidates can give a 3-5 minutes speech if they want about their interest in becoming the Bali Hash 2 Hash Master before the vote
    • The Chair of the Election (Serial Offender) will oversee the vote with an administrative assistant
    • Once voting is complete, the Chair will tally and count the votes which will be validated by the Committee
    • All members of the club will be notified of the election results before the run
    • Start the Run
    • Current Hash Master will hand over to the new Hash Master when the circle starts
  • Nominasi untuk Hash Master
    • Nominasi Hash Master akan dinyatakan dan diperbantukan oleh sesama anggota Bali Hash 2 1 minggu sebelum pemungutan suara (yaitu, paling lambat tanggal 28 Desember 2019)
    • Calon Master Hash tidak boleh menjadi anggota Komite aktif dari klub lain
    • Calon Master Hash harus menjadi Anggota Bali Hash 2 selama minimal 2 tahun
    • Calon Master Hash harus memiliki pengalaman Komite Bali Hash 2 sebelumnya
  • Proses Pemilihan
    • Anggota dengan Bali Hash 2 memenuhi syarat untuk memilih, pengunjung dan perawan tidak berhak untuk memilih
    • Surat suara yang sudah dicetak akan tersedia dengan nama-nama kandidat sebelum pencalonan
    • Anggota luar negeri yang aktif dapat memberikan suara di muka melalui email
      • Kami akan menggunakan daftar kas hash untuk mengembangkan daftar pemilih yang memenuhi syarat
    • Para kandidat dapat memberikan pidato 3-5 menit jika mereka mau untuk menjadi Hash 2 Hash Master Bali sebelum pemungutan suara
    • Ketua Pemilihan Umum (Serial Offender) akan mengawasi pemilihan dengan asisten administrasi
    • Setelah pemungutan suara selesai, Ketua akan menghitung dan yang akan divalidasi oleh Komite
    • Semua anggota klub akan diberitahu tentang hasil pemilihan sebelum pertandingan
    • Mulai Jalankan
    • Master Hash saat ini akan menyerahkan ke Master Hash baru ketika lingkaran dimulai

Rabid Mangy Dog Queen Victoria Bali Hash 2

Queen Victoria (a.k.a. Rabid Mangy Dog) was Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland and Empress of India. The daughter of Prince Edward, Duke of Kent and Strathearn, she was raised under close supervision by her mother, Princess Victoria of Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld. She inherited the throne at the age of 18,  married her first cousin Prince Albert of Saxe-Coburg and Gotha and became a national icon identified with strict standards of morality.

Now anyone who knows Rabid Mangy Dog will also know that one cannot equate Mr Dog with strict standards of morality. However, he is a hashman and will celebrate his 271st birthday in Style this year at Puri Damai in Banjar Tunon with a great run, massage girls on-site, food and other types of goodies and giveaways before putting on a personal performance of Queen Victoria.
