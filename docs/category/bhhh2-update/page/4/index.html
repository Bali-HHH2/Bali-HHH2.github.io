Next Run Map

Last weeks run at Tenganan Pegeringsingan was amazing, this was pretty much the most scenic place I have ever seen in the 11 years of hash running that I have done here in Bali. And that’s no small feat considering all of the places that we have run from. If you EVER get a chance to visit this village of Tenganan Pegeringsingan in Candi Dasa you should definitly do it!

So the run was amazing and the village was so amazing that it actually looked and reminded me of a hobbit village. So we were at this hobbit village in Bali, but with Balinese not hobbits.

The trail and paper were amazing, once we made it to the top of the hill we looked out over a wide expanse of valley with rice terraces all over th eplace. Continuing on, we broke out on the other side of the ridge to an absolutely amazing view overlooking the valley where Tenganan Pegeringsingan was nestled away, with the Indian ocean in the background.

Back at the run site, the t-shirts and food were also amazing.  And the local people who welcomed us warmly to their village for the run were really beyond amazing. They helped us find the trail and on the day even made a bon fire for us because they didn’t want us to be cold!

So I guess your starting to get the point. This was an absolutely AMAZING RUN! Dare I even say it?  Was this a Victor Awards best run of the year amazing run?

Ok so our next run is at the wantilan in Singapadu next to the Bali Zoo.  Easy to find, not far away and a great run site because there are just so many ways that you can go from there. Moaner Groaner will be setting the run with a Virgin Hare so it should be exciting.  So On On to Singapdu this Saturday 21st of July.

Hash Day in Bali Today Saturday July 7th @ Goa Gajah

Come join Team America and Bali Hash House Harriers 2 for the annual United States of America Independence Day run at Goa Gajah. We have a full on celebration lined up with 2 run trails being set around the beautiful Balinese country side starting from the parking lot at the ancient temple site of sacred Elephant Caves. From there we will set off and find a split on the trail for the 6 km short trail and the 10km long trail. We will be celebrating Independence Day at the parking lot with American style food, free flow beer and soft drinks and music.

Upon the conclusion of trail (either by returning to the starting bar or by reaching their destination), hashers are generally given a few minutes break in order to pour themselves a beer, use the restroom, or anything else that they might need to finish before “Circle” is called by the attending Hash Master. The Hash Master then commences with calling in the Hares for a down down, followed by welcoming back of returning hashers and the anointing of Virgin hash runners to Bali Hash House Harriers 2.

Once the formal business is concluded, the RA or Religious Advisor begins the calling of accusations, a calling out of hashers for either ridicule or for praise. When a hasher has been accused, they then come into the middle of the circle and before the entire hash, the accusation is stated, and a song is sung. Upon conclusion of the song, the hasher must then finish their beer or beverage of choice, also referred to as a Down Down. If a hasher does not want to finish their or beverage of choice (for whatever reason), they are permitted to pour the remainder of said or beverage of choice on their head.

It should be noted that there is no defense for accusations, and anyone who is called out for a down-down must come into the circle unless the RA states otherwise.

Good Friends, Good Trail, Good Circle …. Come Join Us Bali Hash House Harriers 2 …. 2 ….. 2!

ANNOUNCEMENT: Bali Hash House Harriers 2 Saturday 17 March 2018

March 6, 2018/0 Comments/in BHHH2 Update /by Bali Hash 2 Hare Raiser

ANNOUNCEMENT from

Bali Hash House Harriers 2

Regarding Saturday 17 March 2018

Dear Hashers,

This Year, Bali’s Day of Silence, “Nyepi”, Saka 1940 of Hindus, falls on Saturday the 17th of March.  As you know, the Balinese celebrate the new year of the Saka calendar with meditation and silence. No activities are allowed Island wide. The religious prohibitions include:

  • no pleasure (amati lelangon),
  • no traffic (amati lelungan),
  • no fire (amati geni) and
  • no work (amati karya).

Since Nyepi falls on a Saturday, Bali Hash House Harriers 2 will not be running as per our usual schedule.  We will therefore have our annual Nyepi run for Saka 1940 on SUNDAY the 18th of March, the day AFTER the Nyepi.

Remember, the silence begins at sunrise around 6:00 am of 17 March 2018 and will go on until sunrise around 6:00 am of 18 March 2018. During that time you should refrain from activity, no TV’s or loud music, no lamps or fires.

This is the only place in the world where the government will shut down an airport for meditation and introspection! The roads and beaches are off limits to all types of motorized vehicles and foot traffic.

While indoors, please ensure that all audio devices are turned down to a minimum volume. As the day draws to an end and the sun sets, curtains need to be drawn shut, with minimum light being used.

We look forward to welcoming you to our Nyepi Run on Sunday the 18th of March!

Sincerely,

Bali Hash House Harriers 2 Mis-management
