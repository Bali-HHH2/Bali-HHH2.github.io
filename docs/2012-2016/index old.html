Notice To Members

An Extraordinary General Meeting will be held at Mertasari Beach Sanur on

Sunday June 26th 2016

As many people are aware our club has not been able to find new members to serve on our committee and, as a result, our club is no longer active. This meeting will be held to form a new committee to run Bali Hash House Harriers 2.

The club is looking for people to fill the following positions:

  • Hash Master
  • Hash Cash
  • Hash Beans (3 positions)
  • Hare Raiser
  • Religious Adviser
  • Hash Boutique
  • Beer Master
  • Hash Scribe

If you wish our hash to continue you should come to the meeting to support the forming of a new committee.

All members are asked to attend, please do not bring non members.

Goals Of The Hash
From the 1938 charter of the Kuala Lumpur Hash House Harriers:

  • To promote physical fitness among our members
  • To get rid of weekend hangovers
  • To acquire a good thirst and to satisfy it in beer
  • To persuade the older members that they are not as old as they feel
